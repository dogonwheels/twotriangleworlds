Two Triangle Worlds

WebGL sandbox for interesting fragment shaders

Inspired by Iñigo Quílez's Rendering Worlds with Two Triangles and starting from the tutorials at Learning WebGL.

  • Simple Quad - Auto sizing WebGL canvas to fill browser window and maintain a minimum (-1, -1) (1, 1) square in view. Interpolates between fixed colors.
  • Mandelbrot - Customary Mandelbrot on the GPU with 50 iteration limit and fixed viewport and color palette.
